Everything you need to know about NovaService

What is novaservice?

Nova Service is a IT orientated platform where you can advertise services to others, from independent users, to small shops, everyone can advertise their service to gain additional work, and using our paid plans, your service will be advertised by novaservice itself, on their social media and app! Of course, you can still use it for free! We aim to create a safe ambient for our clients, thats why we verify your mail, to ensure your safty at any moment.

How to use

Using novaservice is pretty simple, you create a account, then we will send a mail to your marked mail address, to verify you identity. Once you verified the mail address using your code, your account is functional, now you only need to login and you have access to upload posts, advertising your services!
